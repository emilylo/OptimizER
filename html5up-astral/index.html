OptimizER

OptimizER is a tool to optimize non-critical ER and urgent care visits by making care center availability and wait-time information more accessible and by reducing redundancy in communication for both patients and medical care providers. Feel free to enter your symptoms, and we will provide a list of emergency rooms and urgent care centers available and relevant to you. Your symptoms will be saved and made available to your health care providers.

OptimizER is not intended to replace recommendations for critical emergency care. If you are experiencing a serious emergency, please call 911 immediately.
